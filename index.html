About me

I did my Ph.D. in the Titane team at Inria from February 2018 to October 2021, under the supervision of Dr. Florent Lafarge. My Ph.D. was funded by ANR (Agence Nationale de la Recherche).

I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from University of Oxford, UK in 2015, and Master's degree in Applied Maths and Computational Science from KAUST, Saudi Arabia in 2017. During my Master's degree, I worked in the Computational Imaging Group at KAUST, supervised by Dr. Wolfgang Heidrich.


Research Interests

My research interests include geometry processing with applications to 2D&3D computer vision problems, image enhancement, weakly supervised problems.
